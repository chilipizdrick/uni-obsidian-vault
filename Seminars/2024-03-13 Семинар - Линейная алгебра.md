$$\begin{gather}
\mathbb{K}[t] \leftrightarrow \mathbb{K}^{\mathbb{K}} \\
|\mathbb{K}| = q \\
\mathbb{K} [t_{1}, t_{2}, \dots, t_{n} ] \\
\mathbb{K} [t_{1}, t_{2}] = (\mathbb{K} [t_{1}])[t_{2}]
\end{gather}$$

$$\mathbb{K} [t] = a_{0} + a_{1}t + a_{2}t^{2} + \dots + a_{n}t^{n}$$

$$\begin{gather}
\mathbb{K} [t,s] = (\mathbb{K} [t])[s] = \\
= (a_{0} + a_{1}t + a_{2}t^{2} + \dots)s_{0} + (a_{0} + a_{1}t + a_{2}t^{2} + \dots)s_{1} + \\
+ (a_{0} + a_{1}t + a_{2}t^{2} + \dots)s_{2} + \dots + (a_{0} + a_{1}t + a_{2}t^{2} + \dots)s_{n}
\end{gather}$$

> [!note]
> Моном (одночлен) - это жлемент базиса кольца многочленов от нескольких переменных.

$$\mathbb{K} [t_{1}, t_{2}, \dots, t_{n} ] = \sum_{i=1}^{n} a_{k_{1}, k_{2}, \dots, k_{n} }\prod_{j=1}^{n}t_{j}^{k_{j}}$$

$$f: \mathbb{K}^{n} \to \mathbb{K} = \mathbb{K}^{\mathbb{K}^{n}}$$
