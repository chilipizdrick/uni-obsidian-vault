### 53

$$\begin{gather}
(x^{2}-1)y' + 2xy^{2}=0, \ y(0) = 1 \\
\frac{y'}{y^{2}} = -\frac{2x}{x^{2}-1} \\
\int \frac{dy}{y^{2}} = -\int \frac{dx^{2}}{x^{2}-1} \\
\frac{1}{y} = \ln |x^{2}-1| + C \\
y = \frac{1}{\ln |x^{2}-1| + C}, \ C = 1 \\
y = \frac{1}{\ln |x^{2}-1| + 1}
\end{gather}$$
