## 8.132(1)

$$\begin{gather}
z = f(u,v), \ u = xy, \ v = \frac{x}{y} \\
\frac{\partial^{2} z}{\partial x^{2}} = \frac{\partial }{\partial x}\left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial u} \cdot \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial f}{\partial v} \cdot \frac{\partial v}{\partial x} \right) = \\
\frac{\partial}{\partial x} \left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial u} \cdot \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\right) + \frac{\partial}{\partial x} \left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial v} \cdot \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\right) = \\
= \frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial u \partial x}\cdot \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial x^{2}} \cdot \frac{\partial f}{\partial u} + \frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial v\partial x} \cdot \frac{\partial v}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial^{2} v}{\partial x^{2}} \cdot \frac{\partial f}{\partial v} = \\
= \frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial u \partial x}\cdot y + 0 \cdot \frac{\partial f}{\partial u} + \frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial v\partial x} \cdot \frac{1}{y} + 0 \cdot \frac{\partial f}{\partial v} = \\
= y\cdot\frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial u \partial x} + \frac{1}{y} \cdot \frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial v \partial x} = \\
= y^{2} \frac{\partial f}{\partial u} + \frac{1}{y^{2}} \frac{\partial f}{\partial u}
\end{gather}$$ 

$$\begin{gather}
\frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial x^{2}} = \frac{\partial }{\partial x}\left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial u} \cdot \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial f}{\partial v} \cdot \frac{\partial v}{\partial x} \right) = \frac{\partial }{\partial x}\left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial u} \cdot y + \frac{\partial f}{\partial v} \cdot \frac{1}{y} \right) = \\
= y\cdot \frac{\partial^{2} f}{\partial u \partial x} + \frac{1}{y} \cdot \frac{\partial f}{\partial v \partial x} = y\cdot \frac{\partial f}{\partial u} \cdot \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial u} \cdot \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} \right)
\end{gather}$$

## Неявные функции одной и нескольких независимых переменных

$$\begin{gather}
f(x,y) = 0 \\
y'(x) = \frac{dy}{dx} = -\frac{f'_{x}}{f'_{y}} \\
y''(x) = \frac{d}{dx}\left( \frac{dy}{dx} \right) = \frac{d^{2}y}{dx^{2}}
\end{gather}$$

$$\begin{gather}
f(x,y,z) = 0, \ z = z(x,y) \\
\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = - \frac{f'_{x}}{f'_{z}}, \ \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = - \frac{f'_{y}}{f'_{z}} \\
f'_{x}dx + f'_{u}dy + f'_{z}dz = 0 \\
dz = - \frac{f'_{x}dx+f'_{y}dy}{f'_{z}}
\end{gather}$$
