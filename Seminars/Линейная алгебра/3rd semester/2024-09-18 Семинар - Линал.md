## Прошлое занятие

$$\begin{gather}
|x| = |x^{-1}| \ \forall x \in G \\
|y x y^{-1}| = |x| \ \forall x, y \in G \\
|xy| = |yx| \ \forall x,y \in G \\
|x^{k}| = \frac{n}{(n, k)} \\
xy = yx, \ (|x|, |y|) = 1 \implies |xy| = |x||y|
\end{gather}$$

##

$$\begin{gather}
G \to G^{0} \\
x \mapsto x^{-1} \\
GL(n,k) \to GL(n, k)^{0} \\
\begin{cases}
A \mapsto A^{-1} \\
A \mapsto A^{T} \\
\end{cases}
O(n, k) = \{ A \in GL(n,k) \ | \ A^{-1} = A^{T} \}
\end{gather}$$
