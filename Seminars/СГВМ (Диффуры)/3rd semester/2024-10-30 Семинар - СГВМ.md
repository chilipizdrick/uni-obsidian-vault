## 1

$$\begin{gather}
(x^{2}+x)y' = (2x+1)y \\
\ln |y| = \ln |x^{2} + x| + \ln |C| \\
y = C(x^{2}+x), \ C = 1\\
y = x^{2} + x
\end{gather}$$

## 2.4

$$\begin{gather}
yy'' - (y')^{2} = 0, \ y(0) = 1, \ y'(0) = 2 \\
\frac{yy''-(y')^{2}}{(y')^{2}} = 0 \\
-\left( \frac{y}{y'} \right)' = 0 \\
\left( \frac{y}{y'} \right)' = 0 \\
\frac{y}{y'} = C_{1} \\
C_{1} = \frac{y(0)}{y'(0)} = \frac{1}{2} \\
\frac{y}{y'} = \frac{1}{2} \\
\frac{y'}{y} = 2 \\
\ln |y| = 2x+ \ln |C_{2}| \\
y = C_{2}e^{2x} \\
1 = C_{2} e^{2\cdot 0} \\
C_{2} = 1 \\
y = e^{2x} \\
\end{gather}$$
