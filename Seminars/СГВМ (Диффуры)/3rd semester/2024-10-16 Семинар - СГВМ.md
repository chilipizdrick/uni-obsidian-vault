$$\begin{gather}
yy'' = y'(y'+1) \\
\begin{bmatrix}
y' = z(y) \\
y'' = y'z' = z'z
\end{bmatrix} \\
yz'z = z(z+1), \ (z = 0 !) \\
yz' = z+1 \\
y = \frac{z+1}{z'} \\
\frac{1}{y} = \frac{z'}{z+1} \\
\ln |y| = \ln |z+1| + \ln |C_{1}| \\ 
y = z+1 + C_{1} \\
y = y' + 1 + C_{1} \\

\end{gather}$$
