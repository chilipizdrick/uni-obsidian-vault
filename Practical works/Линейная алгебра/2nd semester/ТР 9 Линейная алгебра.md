### $\lambda_{1} = 1$:

$$\begin{gather}
\begin{pmatrix}

\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}

\end{pmatrix} = \vec{0} \implies \vec{x}_{1} = \begin{pmatrix}

-2
\end{pmatrix}, \ |\vec{x}_{1}| = 3
\end{gather}$$

### $\lambda_{2} = 2$:

$$\begin{gather}
\begin{pmatrix}

\end{pmatrix} = \vec{0} \implies \vec{x}_{2} = \begin{pmatrix}
