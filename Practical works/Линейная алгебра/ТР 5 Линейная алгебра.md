### 1. Доказать, что $\hat{A}$ - линейный оператор в М

$$\begin{gather}
1) \ \hat{A}(X+Y) = B(X+Y) - (X+Y)B = BX + BY - XB - YB = \\
= (BX - XB) + (BY - YB) = \hat{A}(X) + \hat{A}(Y)
\end{gather}$$

$$\begin{gather}
2) \hat{A}(\lambda X) = B\lambda X - \lambda XB = \lambda(BX - XB) = \lambda \hat{A}(X)
\end{gather}$$
