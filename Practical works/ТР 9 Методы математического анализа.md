### Вариант 5

$$\begin{gather}
\int_{0}^{1/2} \frac{\arctan x}{x} \, dx \\
\end{gather}$$

$$\begin{gather}
\arctan x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^{n}x^{2n+1}}{2n+1}, \ \forall x \in (-1; 1) \\
\int_{0}^{1/2} \frac{\arctan x}{x} \, dx = \int_{0}^{1/2} \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^{n}x^{2n}}{2n+1} \, dx = \\
= \int_{0}^{1/2}  \, dx 
\end{gather}$$
