$$\int x^{2}\ln(x^{2}+2x+5) \, dx$$

$$\begin{gather}
\int x^{2}\ln(x^{2}+2x+5) \, dx = \frac{x^{3}}{3}\ln(x^{2}+2x+5) - \int \frac{x^{3}}{3} \cdot \frac{2x+2}{x^{2}+2x+5} \, dx = \\
= \frac{x^{3}\ln(x^{2}+2x+5)}{3} - \frac{2}{3} \int \frac{x^{4}+x^{3}}{x^{2}+2x+5} \, dx 
\end{gather}$$
