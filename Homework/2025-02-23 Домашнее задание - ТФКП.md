### 1.3

$$\begin{gather}
\tan iz = \frac{\sin iz}{\cos iz} = -i\frac{e^{i^{2}z} - e^{-i^{2}z}}{e^{i^{2}z} + e^{-i^{2}z}} = -i \frac{e^{-z}-e^{z}}{e^{-z}+e^{z}} = i\frac{\frac{e^{z}-e^{-z}}{2}}{\frac{e^{z}+e^{-z}}{2}} = i \frac{\sinh z}{\cosh z} = i\tanh z
\end{gather}$$
