f(x) = \sin 2x + 2x \cos 2x \\
\begin{bmatrix}
u = 2x
\end{bmatrix} = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^{n}u^{2n+1}}{(2n+1)!} + u\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^{n}u^{2n}}{(2n)!} = \\
= \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^{n}u^{2n+1}}{(2n)!}\left( 1 + \frac{1}{2n+1} \right), \ x \in (-\infty, + \infty)
\end{gather}$$
