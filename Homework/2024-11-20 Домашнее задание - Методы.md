= \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} (-1)^{n}(n+1)(-x^{3})^{n} = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} (-1)^{2n}(n+1)x^{3n} = \\

\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} (n+1)x^{3n}, \ x \in (-1, 1) \\
\end{gather}$$
