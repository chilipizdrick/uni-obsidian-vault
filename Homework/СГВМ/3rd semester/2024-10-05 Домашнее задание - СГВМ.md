## 27

$$\begin{gather}
\ln y = ax + by \\
\frac{y'}{y} = a +by' \\
\frac{y''y - (y')^{2}}{y^{2}} = by'' \\
b = \frac{y''y - (y')^{2}}{y''y^{2}} \\
a = \frac{y'}{y} - y'\left( \frac{y''y - (y')^{2}}{y''y^{2}} \right) \\
\ln y = \left( \frac{y'}{y} - y'\left( \frac{y''y - (y')^{2}}{y''y^{2}} \right) \right) x + y\left( \frac{y''y - (y')^{2}}{y''y^{2}} \right) 
\end{gather}$$
