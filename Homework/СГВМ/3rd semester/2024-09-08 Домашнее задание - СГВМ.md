$$\begin{gather}
\frac{BC}{AC} = \tan BAC \iff \frac{2y}{x} = y' \\
\frac{2y}{y'} = x \\
\frac{y'}{2y} = \frac{1}{x} \\
\frac{1}{2}\int \frac{y'}{y} \, dx = \int \frac{1}{x} \, dx  \\

\end{gather}$$
